Sagar Luitel (born June, 1999), professionally known as Rhino Gaming is a PUBG Streamer and first official Facebook Gaming creator from Nepal. Rhino Gaming started his streaming career in August 2019 when PUBG Mobile was hyped in Nepal. He is one of the oldest Facebook Streamer from Nepal.

After almost two years of Struggle. In the current date Rhino Gaming manages to get 500-1000 live watching in his Gaming Page. He is one of the most active streamer in Nepal. Nepali fans like him because of his good attitude, Rush gameplay and his dedication towards streaming.
